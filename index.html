Thinking like a historian

THIS IS OUR FIRST UNIT, we learned about primary and secondary sources.
Primary sources are pieces of information that are original.
Also there are secondary sources, they are secondhand accounts, or pieces of information that are not from the original source.









Geography

Our Second unit was pretty good, we learned about geography.
We learned about the 5 themes of geography, which are; Location, Place, Region, Movement, and Human Environment Interaction. Location is absolute and relative, while Place is physical and human characteristics. Region is terrain or described similarities you may find in a place and Movement is transportation.
Human environment interaction is the side effect or contact by a living human.
